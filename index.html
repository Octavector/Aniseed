explain elements vs components. components are complete items. Elements are guidelines for speciifc properties which are applied to components but are themsleves not completed items

Box

a box is the foundation compoent for grouping a collection of related elements together, it is used by other components such as modals and cards to create more complex elements.

Card

Do

  • Make sure the entire card is clickable
  • Use badges to provide meta information

Do not

  • Include buttons and links within the card
  • Have more than four in a row
  • Use on a white background

As cards are entirely clickable, they must not contain any buttons within their box

to signify that the entire card is clickable, they require a clear hover and focus state
